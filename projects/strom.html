works | product | strøm

Outil Atelier is a European manufacturer of handheld power tools. Its new leadership team intends to focus on strengthening its brand identity, becoming synonymous with cutting edge technology and craftsmanship in the power hand tool sector.

Outil Atelier has appointed my team to redesign their Strøm tool range. Strøm is the Danish word for “power”, Outil Atelier's flagship range aimed at professional craftspeople. This market category offers professionals the best safety and control and is built for a tough working environment. This tool range is also completely rebuildable to offer users a lifetime of reliability through parts replacement.
